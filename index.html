    Notable locations in Boston

  • Boston Common:

    Boston Common is a large outdoor green space that holds many spaces for anyone to enjoy! From the frog pond where people can ice skate in the winter and the courts where tennis enjoyers can play in the summer, the Boston Common has a place for everyone!

  • Newbury Street:

    Newbury street is one of the most decorated and liveliest streets in Boston! Located in the heart of the Back Bay neighborhood, this long street has a diverse amount of stores and resturants, and occassionally hosts pop up street markets and outdoor events!

  • North End:

    The North End is a small but lively neighborhood, famous for its Italian food. Surrounded by narrow streets and many old and historic buildings, this neighborhood is a great destination to enjoy yummy food and slick architecture!

Famous Resturant: The Salty Pig

Located on the line of famous Boston neighborhoods, Back Bay and South End, the Salty Pig is a neighborhood focused restaurant with an emphasis on charcuterie. Featuring a wide range of "Salty Pig Parts" in the form of house made meats and hand selected cheeses, The Salty Pig's menu is designed to be mixed and matched to create personalized charcuterie boards.
